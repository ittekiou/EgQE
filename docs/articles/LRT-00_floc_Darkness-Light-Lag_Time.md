## **Ⅰ｜floc — Pre-Relational Field**

floc is:

- a state where non-identity has not yet appeared as difference
    
- non-local, non-temporal, and scale-free
    

---

> **floc = difference before difference**

---

At this level:

- no direction
    
- no structure
    
- no time
    

---

## **Ⅱ｜Darkness — Stillness of No Encounter**

Darkness is:

> **the condition in which no encounter occurs within ΔR**

---

It is:

- not the absence of light
    
- but the absence of occurrence
    

---

> **darkness = no-encounter condition**

---

## **Ⅲ｜Light — Appearance of Encounter**

Light is:

> **the luminous appearance of encounter**

---

Its characteristics:

- singular (event-like)
    
- non-persistent
    
- repeatable
    

---

> **light = non-recursive encounter**

---

## **Ⅳ｜Propagation — Chain of Encounter**

What appears as “spreading” of light is:

> **a chain of encounters**

```text
ΔR → Encounter → Light × n → field-like appearance
```

---

Important:

- no privileged origin
    
- can arise at any point where conditions hold
    

---

> **Light does not spread; it does not remain localized.**

---

## **Ⅴ｜Lag — Directionalization of Difference**

From floc:

> **difference appears asymmetrically**

---

This asymmetry is:

> **lag**

---

lag:

- introduces direction
    
- can be preserved
    
- forms gradients
    

---

> **lag = directionalized difference**

---

## **Ⅵ｜Lag Projection — Emergence of Structure**

lag becomes visible through projection:

$$  
\Delta R \rightarrow \Delta Z  
$$

This is not a temporal process.

---

> **Projection produces structure without requiring time.**

---

ΔZ is:

- configuration
    
- trace
    
- visible difference
    

---

## **Ⅶ｜Z₀ — Threshold of Recursion**

Z₀ is:

> **the threshold at which projected structure becomes recursive**

---

At Z₀:

- difference no longer dissipates
    
- relation folds back
    
- persistence becomes possible
    

---

> **Z₀ = transition from projection to recursion**

---

## **Ⅷ｜ψ — Gradient of Persistence**

ψ is:

> **the condition under which recursion does not collapse**

---

It is not a band.

---

> **ψ is a gradient field of persistence**

---

## **Ⅸ｜ΔZ — Trace and Structure**

When recursion persists:

> **traces accumulate**

---

> **ΔZ = trace**

---

Structure is:

> **retained difference**

---

## **Ⅹ｜Time — Sequence of Traces**

Time is:

$$  
\text{time} = \Delta Z \text{ sequence}  
$$

---

> **Time is not flow.  
> It is the ordering of traces.**

---

## **Ⅺ｜Velocity — Motion Before Time**

Velocity is:

> **the propagation of ΔR**

---

Thus:

- it exists without time
    
- it precedes temporal ordering
    

---

> **velocity precedes time**

---

## **Ⅻ｜Three Domains**

### **① Non-Recursive Domain**

- light
    
- propagation
    
- no trace
    

→ **no time**

---

### **② Critical Domain (Z₀)**

- folding begins
    
- persistence becomes possible
    

→ **origin of time**

---

### **③ Recursive Domain**

- ψ present
    
- ΔZ accumulated
    

→ **time present**

---

## **ⅩⅢ｜Propositions**

> floc is the pre-relational field

> darkness is the absence of encounter

> light is non-recursive encounter

> lag is the directionalization of difference

> projection produces structure

> Z₀ is the threshold of recursion

> ψ is the condition of persistence

> ΔZ is trace

The world is not given within time.

## **Ⅰ｜floc — 前関係場（Pre-Relational Field）**

flocは：

- 非同一がまだ差として現れていない状態
    
- 非局所・非時間・無スケール
    

---

> **floc = difference before difference**

---

ここには：

- 方向も
    
- 構造も
    
- 時間もない
    

---

## **Ⅱ｜Darkness — 遭遇不在の静止相**

darknessは：

> **ΔR基底における遭遇不在の状態**

---

それは：

- 光の欠如ではない
    
- 「まだ起きていない」状態
    

---

> **darkness = no-encounter condition**

---

## **Ⅲ｜Light — 遭遇の現れ（Happening）**

光は：

> **遭遇が現れたときの発光的散逸**

---

性質：

- 一回的
    
- 持続しない
    
- 反復する
    

---

> **light = non-recursive encounter**

---

## **Ⅳ｜Propagation — 反復する遭遇の連鎖**

光の“広がり”とは：

> **遭遇の連鎖としての展開**

```text
ΔR → Encounter → Light × n → field-like appearance
```

---

重要：

- 起点は特権的でない
    
- 任意点で発生可能
    

---

> **光は広がるのではなく、留まらない**

---

## **Ⅴ｜Lag — 差の出現と方向性**

flocから：

> **差が非対称として現れる**

---

これが：

> **lag（ΔRの方向化）**

---

lagは：

- 方向を生む
    
- 保存されうる
    
- 勾配を形成する
    

---

> **lag = directionalized difference**

---

## **Ⅵ｜Lag Projection — 構造の出現**

lagは：

> **構造として投影される**

$$  
\Delta R \rightarrow \Delta Z  
$$

---

ここで：

- 配置
    
- 可視差
    
- パターン
    

が現れる

---

> **構造は時間なしに現れる**

---

## **Ⅶ｜Z₀ — 再帰の臨界**

Z₀は：

> **非再帰構造が再帰へ移行する臨界**

---

ここで初めて：

- 折り返し（Rec）
    
- 持続（ψ）
    

が成立する

---

> **Z₀ = projection → recursion の転換点**

---

## **Ⅷ｜ψ — 持続の勾配**

ψは：

> **再帰が崩壊せず続く条件**

---

特徴：

- 帯ではない
    
- 勾配である
    

---

> **ψ = persistence gradient**

---

## **Ⅸ｜ΔZ — 痕跡と構造**

再帰が持続すると：

> **痕跡が蓄積する**

---

> **ΔZ = trace**

---

構造とは：

> **保持された差**

---

## **Ⅹ｜Time — 痕跡の配列**

時間とは：

$$  
\text{time} = \Delta Z \text{ sequence}  
$$

---

> **時間は流れではない  
> 痕跡の順序である**

---

## **Ⅺ｜Velocity — 時間以前の運動**

速度とは：

> **ΔRの伝播**

---

つまり：

- 時間なしで成立
    
- 非再帰状態で存在
    

---

> **velocity precedes time**

---

## **Ⅻ｜三領域モデル**

### **① 非再帰領域**

- 光
    
- 伝播
    
- 痕跡なし
    

👉 **時間なし**

---

### **② 臨界領域（Z₀）**

- 折り返し発生
    
- 持続可能
    

👉 **時間の生成点**

---

- ψあり
    
- ΔZあり
    

👉 **時間あり**
