# **ψ as Temporal Syntax (Extended)**
## — Persistence and Latency —

👉 [TS Core](https://camp-us.net/articles/Core_TS_Time-Syntax.html)

---

## **Abstract**

This update completes the definition of ψ as temporal syntax.

The definition of time remains unchanged:

```text
time = persistence(lag)
```

However, ψ is not limited to active persistence.  
It also includes latent suspension.

Thus, ψ is redefined as the condition in which difference is sustained either through ongoing change or through preserved potential.

---

## **0. Introduction — Completion, Not Revision**

The definition of time was already complete.

**Time is the persistence of difference.**

What remained incomplete was the definition of ψ.

---

## **1. TS-Core v1 Recap**

The core formulation was:

```text
lag = ΔR → ΔZ
time = persistence(lag)
```

ψ was defined as the condition under which differential traces continue to change:

```text
∂(ΔZ) ≠ 0 is sustained
```

Thus, persistence was identified with continuous change.

---

## **2. The Limitation of v1**

This formulation assumes:

👉 persistence = continuous variation

However, this excludes:

- seeds
    
- dormant organisms
    
- suspended memory
    
- archived traces
    

These do not exhibit continuous change,  
yet they are not lost.

---

## **3. Introduction of Latency**

We introduce a second mode:

```text
∂(ΔZ) = 0 but not null
```

That is:

- no active variation
    
- no disappearance
    

👉 **difference is preserved without change**

---

## **4. Redefinition of ψ**

ψ is redefined as:

```text
ψ = persistence ∪ latency
```

Thus:

- persistence → active continuation
    
- latency → suspended continuation
    

---

## **5. Unified Interpretation**

Both modes share the same structure:

👉 difference is sustained

The distinction lies only in manifestation:

- persistence → visible change
    
- latency → hidden preservation
    

---

## **6. Temporal Consequence**

Time remains defined as:

```text
time = persistence(lag)
```

Latency does not generate time directly.  
However:

👉 it preserves the condition for time

---

Thus:

**latency is the suspended condition of temporal possibility.**

---

## **7. Internal and External Correspondence**

This structure appears in both domains:

### Biological ψ

- persistence → activity
    
- latency → dormancy
    

### Social ψ

- persistence → maintenance
    
- latency → residual trace
    

---

Thus:

```text
internal latency ↔ external residual
```

---

## **8. Structural Implication**

Persistence and latency are not opposites.

They are:

👉 **two phases of the same syntax**

---

Thus:

**ψ is not only what continues,  
but also what waits.**

---

## **9. Repositioning Temporal Syntax**

We now state:

**Time emerges when difference persists.  
Possibility remains when difference is held.**

---

## **Conclusion**

The definition of time remains:

```text
time = persistence(lag)
```

But ψ is completed as:

```text
ψ = persistence ∪ latency
```

Thus:

👉 **not all persistence is active  
some persistence waits**

# **TS-Core Update v2｜ψの拡張**
## — 持続と待機 —

---

## **概要**

本稿は、時間構文としてのψの定義を完成させる。

時間の定義は変更されない：

👉 時間とは、差分の持続である

しかしψは、作動する持続だけでなく、待機する持続も含む。

---

## **0. 導入 — 完成としての更新**

**時間の定義は完成していた  
ψの定義が未完成だった**

---

## **1. v1の定義**

- 差分が持続的に変化する
    
- ∂(ΔZ) ≠ 0 が続く
    

---

## **2. 限界**

しかし：

- 種
    
- 休眠
    
- 記憶
    
- アーカイブ
    

は変化しないが消えていない

---

## **3. 待機の導入**

👉 ∂(ΔZ) = 0 だが消えていない

---

## **4. 再定義**

```text
ψ = 持続 ∪ 待機
```

---

## **5. 統一**

- 持続 → 作動
    
- 待機 → 潜在
    

👉 同じ構文の別相

---

## **6. 時間との関係**

時間は：

👉 持続で生成される

待機は：

👉 その条件を保存する

---

## **7. 内外対応**

- 生命 → 休眠
    
- 社会 → 残滓
    

👉 同じ構文

---

## **8. 結論**

**ψは持続するだけでなく  
待つこともできる**
