# **Invisibility of Support**
## — Structural and Political Modes —

---

## **Abstract**

This section analyzes the invisibility of support.

Support does not appear.  
This invisibility is not due to absence, but to operation.

At the same time, support can also be actively concealed.

We distinguish two modes:

- structural invisibility
    
- political invisibility
    

---

## **0｜Introduction — Why Support Does Not Appear**

What we perceive are:

- time
    
- structure
    
- institutions
    
- archives
    

---

Yet:

👉 **what sustains them does not appear**

---

## **1｜Structural Invisibility**

**Definition:**

**Structural invisibility is the condition in which support does not appear because it is fully operative.**

---

What becomes visible is difference:

- imbalance
    
- deviation
    
- disruption
    

---

Support acts by:

- binding difference
    
- stabilizing tension
    

---

Result:

- difference does not surface
    
- imbalance does not accumulate
    

---

👉 **nothing calls attention to support**

---

👉 **what works disappears**

---

## **2｜Two Modes of Invisibility**

Support becomes invisible in two distinct ways:

---

### **(1) Synchronous Invisibility**

- differences are balanced
    
- tensions are absorbed
    
- no deviation emerges
    

---

👉 invisibility through equilibrium

---

### **(2) Backgrounded Invisibility**

- structures dominate perception
    
- support is displaced behind representation
    
- attention is redirected
    

---

👉 invisibility through displacement

---

## **3｜Political Invisibility**

**Definition:**

**Political invisibility is the condition in which support is actively obscured.**

---

Mechanisms include:

- institutional framing
    
- narrative control
    
- symbolic prioritization
    

---

👉 **what is maintained is shown  
what maintains it is concealed**

---

## **4｜Distinction**

It is essential to distinguish:

- structural invisibility → support does not appear because it works
    
- political invisibility → support is hidden
    

---

👉 **these are not the same**

---

## **5｜Conditions of Appearance**

Support becomes visible when:

- maintenance fails
    
- structures collapse
    
- relations break
    

---

👉 **failure reveals support**

---

## **6｜Asymmetry of Visibility**

- functioning → invisible
    
- breakdown → visible
    

---

👉 visibility depends on failure

---

## **Conclusion**

There are two kinds of invisibility:

- that which disappears by functioning
    
- that which is made to disappear
    

---

## ■ Closing Verse

It was not gone

It was working

And some of it

had been hidden

---

## ■ Final Proposition

**Support is invisible both by operation and by concealment.**

# **Invisibility of Support**
## — 構文的不可視と政治的不可視 —

---

## **Abstract**

本節は支えの不可視性を論じる。

支えは見えない。  
それは欠如ではなく、作動の結果である。

さらに支えは、意図的に隠されることもある。

本節は、不可視を二様式として区別する。

---

## **0｜導入 — なぜ支えは見えないのか**

我々が見るのは：

- 時間
    
- 制度
    
- 構造
    
- 記録
    

---

しかし：

👉 **それを支えるものは見えない**

---

## **1｜構文的不可視**

**定義：**  

**構文的不可視とは、支えが完全に作動しているために現れない状態である。**

---

見えるのは差分である：

- 不均衡
    
- ズレ
    
- 破綻
    

---

支えは：

👉 差分を結びつけ  
👉 緊張を均す

---

結果：

👉 **何も現れない**

---

👉 **うまく働いているものは消える**

---

## **2｜不可視の二様式**

### ■ 同期不可視

- 均衡による不可視  
    👉 作動の結果
    

---

### ■ 背景化不可視

- 構造による覆い隠し  
    👉 表象の問題
    

---

## **3｜政治的不可視**

**定義：**  

**政治的不可視とは、支えが意図的に隠される状態である。**

---

メカニズム：

- 制度
    
- 言説
    
- 記号
    

---

👉 **支えは見えなくされる**

---

## **4｜区別**

- 構文的不可視：働いているため見えない
    
- 政治的不可視：隠されて見えない
    

---

👉 **この二つは異なる**

---

## **5｜可視化条件**

支えは：

👉 **壊れたときに現れる**

---

## **6｜結論**

**見えないものは二つある  
見えないものと  
見えなくされたもの**

---

> 消えていたのではない
> 
> 働いていたのだ
> 
> そして
> 
> 隠されていたものも
> 
> あった
