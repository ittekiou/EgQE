近代思想は、世界の基礎をどこに置くかという問いから始まった。

それは **support の背景化（backgrounding of support）** である。

## 1 落下の発見

近代自然科学の出発点の一つは、Galileo Galilei による落下の研究である。

ガリレオは運動を次のように理解した。

```text

```

落下は単なる出来事ではない。  
それは、運動の基本形である。

後にニュートン力学や軌道理論によって明らかになるように、惑星の軌道もまた **持続する落下**として理解できる。

```text
orbit = stabilized falling
```

落下は関係の運動として現れる。

## 2 デカルトの跳躍

しかしこの命題の成立には、ある構造的操作がある。  
それは主体を成立させている関係や支えが、理論の背景へと退けられていることである。

構造的に書けば次のようになる。

```text
relations → background
support   → background
↓

```

主体は関係の中で成立する存在ではなく、自己自身から立ち上がる **実体**として理解された。

### 空間の実体化

Isaac Newton は、自然の運動を記述するために **absolute space** を導入した。

この空間は物体や関係とは独立した基盤として存在する。

ここでも同じ構造が見られる。

```text
relations → background
support   → background
↓
space = ground
```

空間は関係の結果ではなく、構造の基礎として **実体化**された。

# The Backgrounding of Support in Modern Thought
### From Galileo to Descartes and Newton

Modern thought begins with a question:  
_Where is the ground of the world?_

The answers given in early modern philosophy and science produced two decisive conceptual leaps. Yet both leaps share a common operation: the **backgrounding of support**.

---

## 1 The discovery of falling

One of the origins of modern science lies in the work of Galileo Galilei.

Galileo revealed that motion in nature can be understood through the phenomenon of falling. Later developments in celestial mechanics would show that planetary motion itself can be interpreted as a persistent form of falling.

```
orbit = stabilized falling
```

Falling thus appears not merely as an event but as a relational movement.

---

## 2 The Cartesian leap: the substance of subject

René Descartes sought an indubitable foundation for knowledge. His answer is famously expressed as:

**Cogito ergo sum.**

I think, therefore I am.

However, this move relies on a structural operation: the relations and supports that make thinking possible are pushed into the background.

```
relations → background
support   → background
↓
cogito = ground
```

The subject emerges as a self-grounding substance.

---

## 3 The Newtonian leap: the substance of space

Isaac Newton introduced the concept of **absolute space** as the framework within which motion occurs.

Space in Newtonian physics exists independently of objects and relations.

The same structural operation appears again:

```
relations → background
support   → background
↓
space = ground
```

Space becomes a foundational substance.

---

## 4 The dual ground of modernity

Through these two moves, modern thought establishes two fundamental grounds:

```
subject
space
```

or more precisely:

```
cogito
absolute space
```

Both arise from the same operation: the backgrounding of **support relations**.

---

## 5 The missing layer

Structures do not arise directly from relations alone.  
Rather, they emerge through a stabilizing layer:

```
relation
↓
support
↓
structure
```

Relations become stabilized as supports, and structures arise from these stabilized relations.

Modern thought, however, effectively erased this intermediate layer, reducing the process to:

```
relation → structure
```

This reduction allowed both subject and space to be understood as substances.

---

## 6 Repositioning the history of theory

Seen from this perspective, the conceptual development of modern thought can be reconfigured as follows:

```
Galileo   fall
Leibniz   relation
Descartes subject
Newton    space
Einstein  dynamic structure
HEG       support relations
```

---

## 7 Recovering support

The proposal suggested here is simple: the missing layer must be restored.

```text
fall
↓
relation
↓
support
↓
structure
```

Support relations stabilize relational processes and allow structures to emerge.

For example, gravity can be interpreted as:

```
gravity = stabilized falling relations
```

When falling relations become stabilized, structures such as space-time appear.

---

## 8 Conclusion

Modern thought was made possible by forgetting support.

```
Modern thought forgot support.
```

Recovering this forgotten layer allows us to reinterpret the emergence of structure in relational terms.

```
support makes structure possible
```

And existence itself can be reformulated accordingly:

**Sustineor ergo sum.**  
_Supported, therefore I am._

> We keep falling with lag.
