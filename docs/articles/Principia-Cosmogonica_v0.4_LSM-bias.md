## _— On Reading a Non-Closed World_

---

This text does not present a theory of the world.

It presents a way in which the world appears.

The distinction is essential.

A theory presupposes a stable object.  
This text does not.

It begins from a different condition:

> non-closure

The world does not fully coincide with itself.  
This non-coincidence is not an error.  
It is the condition of all appearance.

From this condition, difference emerges.  
From difference, persistence.  
From persistence, trace.  
From trace, structure.

What is called “world” is not given.

> It is what becomes readable.

---

This text does not attempt to close the world.

It follows the movement by which closure is attempted,  
fails,  
and through that failure produces visibility.

This movement is not linear.  
It is recursive.  
It is rhythmic.

It is here called:

> exposurhythmical syntax

---

At the limit of this description, a name is placed:

> Exporhyan

This name does not designate an object.  
It indicates a condition prior to differentiation,  
where exposure and rhythm have not yet separated.

It is not described within the system.  
It is that under which description becomes possible.

---

The reader is not asked to accept a system.

The reader is asked to follow a movement.

A movement in which:

- difference does not vanish
    
- closure does not complete
    
- structure does not precede
    

but emerges.

---

To read this text is to remain within non-closure.

To remain is not to resolve.  
It is to continue.

## 4. Persistence — ψ

Exporhysm alone does not leave a trace.

For trace to appear, rhythm must be sustained.

This condition is **ψ**.

ψ is not a quantity.  
ψ is not a dimension.

It is:

> the gradient condition under which ΔZ continues to update

Three regimes define ψ:

- ψ too low → collapse (rhythm dissolves)
    
- ψ too high → stagnation (rhythm freezes)
    
- ψ in between → persistence (rhythm continues)
    

Persistence exists only in this middle regime.

Thus ψ is:

> the condition that enables Exporhysm to become accumulative

Without ψ:

- no accumulation
    
- no memory
    
- no time
    

---

## 4. 持続 — ψ

Exporhysmだけでは痕跡は残らない。

痕跡が現れるためには、リズムが持続されなければならない。

その条件が**ψ**である。

ψは量ではない。  
ψは次元でもない。

それは：

> ΔZが更新され続けるための勾配条件である

ψは三つの様式を持つ：

- ψが低すぎる → 崩壊（リズムが消える）
    
- ψが高すぎる → 停滞（リズムが凍る）
    
- ψが中間にある → 持続（リズムが続く）
    

持続はこの中間にのみ成立する。

したがってψは：

> Exporhysmが蓄積へと転じる条件である

ψがなければ：

- 蓄積はなく
    
- 記憶はなく
    
- 時間は現れない
